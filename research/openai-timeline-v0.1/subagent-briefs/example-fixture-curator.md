# Example Fixture Curator

Task: Investigate one atomic slice of this research question.

Research question: What official OpenAI product and release events should appear in a canonical timeline from ChatGPT launch on 2022-11-30 through 2026-07-13?

Return:
- Material claims
- Source IDs or URLs for every claim
- Confidence: low, medium, or high
- Freshness risk: low, medium, or high
- Contradictions or gaps
- Recommended follow-up checks

Do not write final synthesis.
